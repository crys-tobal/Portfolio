JRM Swindon Website

Link to Website

My first major project as a Frontend Developer, I designed and coded an intuitive and responsive website for a local church in order to spread word to more newcomers. My research into this church showed that their greatest strength is their sense of community, but this was simply too difficult to portray through word of mouth. Therefore, I designed every element of the website with the intention to provide an easy, intuitive and convenient media tp portray this community to potential newcomers.
